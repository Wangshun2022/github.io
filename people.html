Faculty

Shun Wang | 王顺

Doctor

He received his Ph.D. degree in optical engineering from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST), Wuhan, China, in 2016. After that he joined Wuhan Institute of Technology. He was a Research Fellow with the School of Civil and Environmental Engineering, Nanyang Technological University(NTU) since 2021. He is currently an associate proffessor in Guangdong University of Technology. His current research interests include optical fiber sensing, structural health monitoring, distributed fiber optic sensing.
